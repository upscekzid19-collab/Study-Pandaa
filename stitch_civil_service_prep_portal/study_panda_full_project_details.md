# Study Panda - Project Overview

Study Panda is a high-fidelity educational platform designed for UPSC and JKPSC aspirants. The app provides a premium, focused learning experience with a robust student interface and a powerful administrative management portal.

## 🎨 Visual Identity & Brand
- **Brand Name:** Study Panda
- **Mascot:** A scholarly panda wearing glasses and a graduation cap, symbolizing focus and excellence.
- **Primary Aesthetic:** "Institutional Excellence Dark" – a professional navy blue and gold palette optimized for long study sessions.
- **Typography:** Public Sans (clean, highly readable sans-serif).

---

## 🎓 Student Experience (Mobile App)
The student interface is designed for high utility, engagement, and content mastery.

### 1. Authentication & Onboarding
- **User Login:** Features a circular branded logo, email/password authentication, and social login (Google/Apple).
- **Home Screen:** The central hub featuring:
    - **Daily Streak:** Gamified learning progress.
    - **Current Affairs:** Daily summaries of critical news.
    - **Exam Selection:** Toggle between UPSC and JKPSC.
    - **Quick Links:** Fast access to Live Sessions, Mock Tests, and Study Material.

### 2. Learning Modules
- **Videos Section:** Categorized video lectures with search and progress tracking.
- **Video Player:** An immersive player with options to download for offline use, save for later, and a "Take Notes" feature. Includes recommended next lectures.
- **PDFs Section:** Organized repository for study materials, previous year papers, and specialized notes.

### 3. Assessment & Progress
- **Quiz & Tests:** Daily 5-MCQ challenges, subject-specific tests, and full-length mock examinations.
- **Progress Dashboard:** Visual data on streak history, course completion percentages, and performance analytics.
- **User Profile:** Manage subscription status, account details, and app preferences.

## 🛠️ Technical Implementation
- **Framework:** Production-ready HTML5 and Tailwind CSS.
- **Design System:** Token-based styling for consistent spacing, colors, and interactive states.
- **Asset Management:** All brand assets (logos, icons) are integrated directly into the code via secure DataStore placeholders.
